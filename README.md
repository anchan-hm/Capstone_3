# **EmporiUm East Region Sales Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)**



### **Project Overview:**



For this project, I have been assigned to analyze four years of sales data for the EmporiUm **East Region**, which includes the states of **New York** and **Connecticut**. The goal is to break down how the East Region performed over time, identify important sales patterns, and analyze results by category, state, and product type to help the team understand recent performance and future opportunities.



The analysis was completed using **Power BI** to create a report that highlights key insights such as sales trends, category performance, state-level results, and top-selling books and their authors. This report is designed to support the regional sales team in making informed decisions and preparing for upcoming sales cycles.



### **Key Points From Analysis:**

* Reviewed four years of sales trends to identify seasonal patterns and overall growth.
* Analyzed category performance to see which product groups drive the most revenue.
* Compared sales between New York and Connecticut to understand each state's contribution.
* Identified top-selling books and their authors in the East Region (excluding textbooks).
* Highlighted insights that can help the sales team focus on strong categories and future opportunities.



### **Tools \& Technologies Used:**

* Power BI Desktop
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Power Query (M Language)
* Data Modeling (Star Schema)
* Excel/CSV Files
* Interactive Visuals (Line Charts, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Maps, KPIs, Treemaps)
